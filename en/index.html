Overview

For the past 5 years, LLG students have been organizing an inter-class volleyball tournament. It is a highly anticipated event by all participants, held in 2 phases:

  • A pool phase where classes at the same level compete and the best teams are qualified
  • An elimination phase where qualified classes play in a round of 16 to crown the champion class

This year (2025-2026), the tournament has 33 registered teams:
  • 7 (/7) sophomore classes
  • 7 (/9) junior classes
  • 8 (/9) senior classes
  • 11 CPGE classes
